Introduction

When a student is unable to score well, many problems arise in the student’s mind, whether he can achieve his ambition
and whether he can take care of his family. These questions also pop up in the parent’s minds. There are many reasons
why a student is unable to score well. So we have made a website which creates a timetable based on
sleeping hours, playtime, food, and sports. He/she might face these issues -

  • Improper Sleep
  • Lack of nutritious food
  • Lack of proper outdoor activities
  • Parental Pressure
  • Lack of commitment
  • Lack of goals
  • Improper time management
  • Lack of motivation and effort

sleep data

Know your number of sleep hours required

Many students do not sleep properly. Thus, they cannot concentrate in class. The minimum time for teenagers
is 8-10 per day. This will give the mind and body rest for enough time and you stay alert during classes.
